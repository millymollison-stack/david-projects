# Second Brain System - Based on Tiago Forte's PARA Method

## The Four Categories

### P - Projects
Short-term efforts in your work or life that you want to organize, accomplish, or complete.
- Have a defined outcome
- Have a deadline
- Have next action

### A - Areas
Sphere of activity with standards to maintain over time.
- No deadline
- Ongoing responsibility
- Examples: Health, Finances, Family

### R - Resources
Topics of interest that may be useful in the future.
- Reference materials
- Research
- Interests

### S - Archives
Inactive items from the other three categories.
- Completed projects
- Inactive areas
- Old resources

## Code Capture Workflow

1. **Capture** - Collect interesting/useful information
2. **Organize** - Place in appropriate PARA category
3. **Distill** - Extract the essential points
4. **Express** - Create something from the knowledge
